ABOUT US






MASS COATING is a partnership firm formed since 15th Feb 2016. The company’s registered office is at Bhosari,Pune. The firm is engaged in offering Shot Blasting, Powder Coating and Painting job work to automotive, earthmoving & agricultural components for export and domestic business. Our plant is at Khalumbre (Chakan) Industrial Area.
