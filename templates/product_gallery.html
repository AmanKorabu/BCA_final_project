About Us

MASS COATING is a partnership firm formed since 15th Feb 2016. The company's registered office is at Bhosari, Pune. The firm is engaged in offering Shot blasting, Powder Coating and Painting job work to automotive, earthmoving, and agricultural components for export and domestic business. Our plant is at Khalumbare, (Chakan) industrial area.
